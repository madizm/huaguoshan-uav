# Separate W/G Candidate and Suitable Airspace Layers

We will model W/G candidate airspace separately from suitable airspace. W/G candidates are height-derived business display zones, with W covering [0,120)m AGL and G covering [120,300]m AGL, while suitable airspace is generated by intersecting those candidates with the two-dimensional Airspace Suitability Footprint that already reflects exclusions such as B/C controlled airport airspace.

## Considered Options

- Treat W/G candidates as the final suitable airspace.
- Generate W/G only inside the suitability footprint.
- Keep W/G candidates independent, then derive suitable airspace by overlaying the suitability footprint.

## Consequences

The frontend can independently show W candidates, G candidates, W suitable airspace, G suitable airspace, and mixed boundary cells. The generation pipeline must publish separate `candidate` and `suitable` 3D Tiles outputs and preserve candidate class metadata in suitable cells.
